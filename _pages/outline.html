Description

A survey of critical theory from Karl Marx to Karen Barad to examine the articulation of media and materiality since the 1850s, with an emphasis on questions of agency

Aims

To develop a granular sense of how media and materiality have changed over time and then translate that knowledge across a spectrum of scholarly action

Assignments

A portfolio of eight response papers (to be revised at the term's end), together with two brief presentations and facilitations of seminar discussion
